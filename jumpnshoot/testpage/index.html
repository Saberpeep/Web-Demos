Overview

These mystical sigils keep the world in balance. They signify the four glorious goals of man. Their designs come from the rich Japanese culture, originally used in a similar manner to family crests.

Sigils are primarily acquired through the Syndicates system, with the first Sigil being gifted upon being initiated into a particular group, and further Sigil designs becoming available as one attains favor with said group. Sigils play a very important part in the Syndicates system, as wearing a Syndicate's sigil when in public grants sigil bearers Standing with them, allowing a sigil bearer to rise through the ranks to gain rewards. The higher a Sigil's rank, the larger the respect gained, making wearing said Sigils more effective at acquiring Standing.
