Use [A][D] or [←][→] keys to walk left and right

Hold the [shift] key while walking to run

Use the [space], [W], or [↑] key to jump

Hold the [S] or [↓] key to fall down through platforms

Press the jump key while in the air to double-jump

aim with the mouse, Hold the left mouse button to shoot

Most text-level elements are shootable. Elements will break after being hit with enough bullets
